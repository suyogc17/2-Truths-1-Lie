What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently remove:

  • Your account record (email, display name, sign-in provider)
  • Your game telemetry (scores, answers, streaks, stats)
  • Your profile data and preferences
  • Any device-level identifiers tied to your account

Some data may be retained for a short period to comply with legal obligations (fraud prevention, regulatory requests). This data is not used for any product or marketing purpose and is deleted as soon as the legal requirement allows.
