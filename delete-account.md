## How to delete your account and data

We make it simple. Email us and we'll handle it within 30 days.

### Step 1 — Send us an email

Email **thequizblend@gmail.com** with the subject line:

> **Delete my account**

### Step 2 — Include this in the email body

If you signed up with **email and password**:
- Send the email *from* the same email address you use to sign in,
  **or** include that email address in the body.

If you signed up with **Google Sign-In**:
- Send the email *from* the same Google account you used to sign in,
  **or** include the Google email in the body.

If you played **as a guest** (no sign-up):
- Just uninstall the app — that's it. There's no account to delete
  on the server side. Anonymous telemetry is automatically purged
  after 90 days of inactivity.

### Step 3 — We confirm

You'll receive a confirmation email once your data has been deleted.
This typically happens within 7 business days but is guaranteed
within 30 days.

---

## What gets deleted

When you request account deletion, we permanently remove:

- Your account record (email, display name, sign-in provider)
- Your game telemetry (scores, answers, streaks, stats)
- Your profile data and preferences
- Any device-level identifiers tied to your account

Some data **may be retained** for a short period to comply with legal
obligations (fraud prevention, regulatory requests). This data is not
used for any product or marketing purpose and is deleted as soon as
the legal requirement allows.
