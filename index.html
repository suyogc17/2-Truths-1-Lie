5. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your country, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Object to certain uses of your data
  • Withdraw consent for advertising or analytics

To exercise any of these rights, email us at thequizblend@gmail.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

To delete your account:

  • Email/Google sign-in users: Email us at thequizblend@gmail.com from your sign-in email address (or include it in the message body) with the subject line "Delete my account". We will permanently delete your account and all associated data within 30 days of receiving the request and send you a confirmation when deletion is complete.
  • Guest (anonymous) users: Uninstall the App to remove all local data. Anonymous server-side telemetry will be deleted automatically after 90 days of inactivity.

In-app account deletion will be added in a future update. Until then, the email process above is the official method. See the dedicated Delete Account page for step-by-step instructions.

Region-specific rights

  • EU/EEA/UK (GDPR): You have the right to data portability and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
  • California (CCPA/CPRA): You have the right to know, delete, and opt out of "sale" of personal information. We do not sell personal information.
  • India (DPDP Act): You have the right to access, correction, erasure, and grievance redressal.
