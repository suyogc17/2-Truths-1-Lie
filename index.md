## 2. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to run the game, save your progress, and
keep the experience free through ads. We do **not** sell personal data
to anyone.

### 2.1 Information you provide directly

| Data | When we collect it | Why |
|---|---|---|
| Email address | If you sign in with Email/Password | Account identity, sign-in |
| Display name (chosen IGN) | When you set your in-game name | Shown on game screens |
| Google account info (name, email, profile photo URL) | If you choose Sign in with Google | Account identity, sign-in |

You can also choose to play **as a guest** (anonymous), in which case
none of the above is collected.

### 2.2 Information collected automatically

| Data | Purpose |
|---|---|
| Game telemetry — your score, answer choices, time taken per question, difficulty progression | Improve question pool, balance difficulty, calculate stats |
| Device information — device type, OS version, app version, language, timezone | Diagnose crashes, ensure compatibility |
| Crash logs | Identify and fix bugs |
| Anonymous user ID (Firebase UID) | Tie your progress to your account or device |

### 2.3 Information collected for advertising

We use **Google AdMob** to show advertisements. AdMob may collect:

- Your device's advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID)
- IP address
- General location (country/region, derived from IP)
- Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks, completions)

You can reset or limit your advertising ID at any time in your device
settings (Android: Settings → Privacy → Ads).

### 2.4 What we do **not** collect

- Phone contacts
- Photos, camera or microphone access
- Precise location (GPS)
- Browsing history outside the App
- Health, financial, or biometric data
